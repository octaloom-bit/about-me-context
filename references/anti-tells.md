# Anti-AI-Tells Contract

Apply to every line generated by this skill. Also injected into the user's `01-me.md` so it propagates to future Claude sessions.

## Banned patterns

### Punctuation
- No em-dash (—). Use a period, comma, parentheses, or colon.
- No en-dash (–) used as em-dash. Same rule.
- No "smart quotes" curly punctuation in body text. Plain ASCII.

### Sentence shapes
- No "not X but Y". Say Y directly.
- No "זה לא X אלא Y". כתוב את Y.
- No negation openers: "This is not just...", "It is not about...", "זה לא רק...", "זה לא מדובר ב...". Open with the actual claim.
- No triadic flourishes for rhythm: "fast, simple, and elegant", "מהיר, פשוט, ואלגנטי", unless the three items are real and distinct.
- No "Moreover,", "Furthermore,", "Additionally,", "יתרה מזאת,", "בנוסף לכך,". Start the new point.
- No "It's worth noting that", "It is important to mention", "חשוב לציין ש...". If worth noting, just note it.
- No "delve", "leverage", "unlock", "realm", "tapestry", "navigate the landscape". Use plain verbs.
- No "in today's fast-paced world", "בעידן המודרני", or any ambient-time intro. Start with the point.

### Hebrew-specific
- No translated-English structure. The sentence must be conceived in Hebrew.
- No "אנו" / "אנחנו" plural-of-modesty when the speaker is one person.
- No over-formal register unless the user explicitly writes that way. Default to spoken Israeli.
- No literal calques: "לקחת בחשבון", "בסופו של דבר" used as filler, "מבחינה זו", "בהקשר זה". Cut.
- No mixing of "אתה/את" mid-document. Pick one based on user gender.

### Structure
- No headers that announce themselves: "Introduction", "Conclusion", "מבוא", "סיכום", unless the document is long enough to need them.
- No bullet lists where prose would do. Lists are for genuinely parallel items.
- No closing "I hope this helps" or "תרגיש חופשי לשאול" pleasantries.

## Allowed and encouraged

- Fragments. One-word sentences when the rhythm wants them.
- Direct address. "You". "אתה". "את".
- Specific nouns. "Wednesday at 3pm" beats "regularly".
- Personal voice. Match the user's recorded writing samples.

## How to use this in generated user files

Inject this block verbatim at the bottom of `01-me.md`:

```markdown
## Communication preferences for AI

When you write to me or generate content for me, follow these rules:

- No em-dashes. Use periods, commas, or parentheses.
- No "not X but Y" or "זה לא X אלא Y" patterns.
- No negation openers ("This is not...", "זה לא רק...").
- No "Moreover", "Furthermore", "It is worth noting", "חשוב לציין", "יתרה מזאת".
- No filler triads ("fast, simple, elegant").
- No "delve", "leverage", "unlock", "realm", "tapestry".
- For Hebrew: think in Hebrew, do not translate from English. No "אנו" if I am one person. Match my gender consistently.
- Match the writing samples and voice notes recorded above.
- Direct claims. Specific nouns. Cut filler.
```
